## 📖 2.1 Existing Systems

Currently, information on bus routes in Dhaka is disseminated through a fragmented mix of sources, including:

**🚌 Manually Painted Bus Signs**
  - Buses display route information using manually painted signs (often in Bengali). However, these signs can be difficult to read, are not standardized, and usually do not include all bus stops.

**🗣️ Word-of-Mouth**
  - The most common method where passengers rely on local shopkeepers, rickshaw pullers, or fellow commuters for route information. This method is highly unreliable.

**🌐 Online Forums and Social Media**
  - Platforms such as Facebook groups are frequently used to ask for bus route information. However, the information is unstructured and often requires time to receive responses.

**📱 Mobile Applications**
  - Several bus route applications have been developed, but many are outdated, provide limited coverage, lack user-friendly interfaces, or are no longer actively maintained.

---

## 📲 2.2 Related Applications

Several applications exist globally and locally that attempt to solve similar transportation problems.

### 🗺️ Google Maps

Google Maps is one of the most widely used navigation applications worldwide. It provides public transit directions, including bus routes in many cities.

However:

- Coverage of Dhaka's complex bus network is often incomplete.
- Local bus routes and fare information are frequently unavailable or inaccurate.
- It is designed as a general-purpose navigation platform rather than a dedicated Dhaka bus information system.

---

### 🚖 Pathao / Uber

Although primarily ride-sharing platforms, Pathao and Uber have introduced limited public transit features.

However:

- They are not focused on providing a comprehensive public bus information system.
- Their primary services remain ride-hailing rather than public transportation guidance.

## ⚖️ 2.4 Strengths and Weaknesses of Existing Approaches

### ✅ Strengths

#### 🗺️ Google Maps

- Powerful global mapping and routing algorithms.
- Highly polished and familiar user interface.
- Widely adopted by users around the world.

---

#### 🌐 Social Media

- Provides community-driven information.
- Can occasionally offer updates regarding temporary route changes.

---

### ❌ Weaknesses

#### 🗺️ Google Maps

- Lacks detailed local bus stop and fare information for Dhaka.
- Not designed to handle the unique complexities of Dhaka's bus network.

---

#### 🌐 Social Media

- Information is unstructured.
- Difficult to verify accuracy.
- Requires considerable effort to extract useful information.
- Reactive rather than proactive.

---

#### 🗣️ Word-of-Mouth

- Highly unreliable.
- Information is often inaccurate or outdated.

---

#### ⚠️ General Absence

- There is currently no single, reliable, comprehensive, and up-to-date platform dedicated to Dhaka's public bus information.

---

## 🔍 2.5 Research Gap

The analysis of existing systems reveals a significant gap in Dhaka's public transportation information services.

There is currently **no dedicated, reliable, and user-friendly platform** that provides comprehensive bus route and fare information specifically for Dhaka.

Although Google Maps offers powerful navigation capabilities, it lacks detailed localized data for Dhaka's bus network. Other available sources are either unreliable or do not maintain a structured and regularly updated database.

This project addresses this gap by providing a dedicated solution that combines a user-friendly interface with a robust and manageable database.

## 💡 2.6 Why this Project is Needed

This project is needed to bridge the information gap in Dhaka's public transportation system. It directly addresses the daily challenges faced by millions of commuters.

The application aims to:

1. **Empower Commuters**
   - Enable users to make informed travel decisions.

2. **Improve Efficiency**
   - Reduce the time spent searching for the correct bus.

3. **Enhance Transparency**
   - Provide accurate and upfront fare information.

4. **Digitize a Key Public Service**
   - Support the modernization of Dhaka's public transportation system.

5. **Serve as a Foundation**
   - Establish a scalable platform that can be expanded with future features such as live bus tracking, route optimization, and transportation analytics.
